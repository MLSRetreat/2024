7.-9. August 2024 at Rigi Kaltbad.

On behalf of your Retreat Organizing Committee 2024, we are excited to bring you the next MLS retreat!

We will meet under the angel in the main hall of Zurich HB on 07.08.2024 at 8 am.


From there, we will take the train together to Rigi Kaltbad. For a hike, please bring proper shoes. For the spa, don't forget your swim wear! Also, pack sunscreen, your poster and a notebook! Over 3 days, you will hear some great keynote talks and some awesome flash talks by your own peers, alongside interactive poster and networking sessions.
